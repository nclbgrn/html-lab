Previous Works

I will provide a carefully chosen collection of my work that highlights my experiences as a SAIT interactive design student. Every project showcases how I've addressed different design issues and investigates my abilities and creativity. Throughout the whole design process, from the first brainstorming meeting to the last execution, I have been fully involved. In my opinion, good design appeals to people's emotions and fulfills practical needs. I've learned a lot about the fundamentals of user-centered design by working on these projects, and I now make it my goal to develop experiences that are not just aesthetically pleasing but also simple to use and captivating.
